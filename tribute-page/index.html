Pikachu Tribute (1991 - 2000)

The Pokemon that inspired a billion Pokemon trainers

Pikachu was one of the most famous Pokemon, loved by fans across the world. He was Ash Ketchum's best friend and the most courageous fighter. Unfortunately he got yeeted by another pokemon in the movie and "died" (spoilers: he didn't actually die, Ash just had a cry about him and he came back to life).

Here are some of the amazing achievements Pikachu over his lifetime:

  • 1991 - was born
  • 1992 - didn't evolve but still kicked ass
  • 1996 - helped Ash beat all the gyms and advanced to the league
  • 1997 - electrocuted Gyrados and won the Pokemon battle
  • 1997 - nearly evolved but got lazy and stopped halfway through the evolution.
  • 1998 - ate a cranberry.
  • 1999 - dominated every gym in the Hoen region.
